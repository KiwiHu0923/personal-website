Overview

This app was designed to meet the specific needs of tracking and managing TV series and movies. It offers comprehensize and detailed recording and categorization capabilities, focusing on aspects such as OTT platforms, countries, actors and more.

The architecture of the app is designed to ensure efficient database management, tracking and recording viewing habits.

Features

  • Comprehensive Media Categories and Description

    First, the homepage is divided into Shows, Movies, Upcoming Shows, and Upcoming Movies, allowing users to easily see the information, ratings and upcoming releases.

    User can also view detailed media description, including posters, OTT platforms, cast lists, release date and regions and more.

  • Cursomized Watchlists, Ratings, and Viewing Statistics

    The app offers a service to add unwatched media to a watchlist and allows users to rate each movie they have already seen.

    Additionally, the app automatically ranks actors based on the number of times they have appeared in the movies one have watched.

  • Dynamic Calendar View for Watched and Upcoming Media

    A scrollable calendar view presents daily viewing records and detailed information about upcoming movies, allowing users to easily navigate through their watch history and future releases.

  • Accurate Viewing Time Statistics

    The app provides precise statistics on daily, monthly, and yearly viewing times, as well as comparisons of viewing times across different streaming platforms.

Architecture

  1. Data Management

    MySQL is used as a local database for storing and manipulating data. For example, rating media, tracking viewing habits, and organizing playlists and more.

  2. Call SQL query in SwiftUI

    Write SQL queries in JavaScript so that I can leverage its flexibility as the intermediary between the app and database, to handle database operation in SwiftUI.

  3. Fetch media information from website

    The app integrates with the TMDB API to fetch a wide range of media content from the The Movie Database website.

Technologies

  • SwiftUI
  • Express.js
  • MySQL
